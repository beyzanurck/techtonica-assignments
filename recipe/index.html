Beyza's Awesome Recipe

This mouthwatering dish combines traditional Italian pasta with zesty Pesto sauce, creating a taste sensation like no other.

Photo Credit: A Couple Cooks

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz (340g) pasta of your choice (penne, fusilli, or farfalle)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup toasted pine nuts (or walnuts)
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, roasted vegetables, extra Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor or blender, combine the fresh basil, grated Parmesan cheese, toasted pine nuts, and garlic. Pulse the mixture until it forms a coarse paste.
  3. While the food processor is running, slowly drizzle in the extra-virgin olive oil until the pesto reaches your desired consistency. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked pasta with the freshly made pesto sauce. Toss the pasta gently until it is well coated with the pesto.
  5. Optional: Add cherry tomatoes, roasted vegetables, or extra grated Parmesan cheese on top for additional flavor and texture.
  6. Serve the Pesto Pasta Perfection on plates and enjoy immediately!
